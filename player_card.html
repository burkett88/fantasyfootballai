Key Changes

The Chiefs have bolstered their offensive line by signing Jaylon Moore to a $15 million per year contract to address protection issues. (draftsharks.com) Additionally, wide receivers Rashee Rice and Hollywood Brown are returning from injuries, aiming to enhance the passing game. (espn.com)

Outlook

With a healthier receiving corps and improved offensive line, Mahomes is poised for a more productive season. However, the shift towards a more conservative, run-based offense may limit his fantasy upside. (fantasyalarm.com) While still a top-tier quarterback, his fantasy production may not reach previous peaks.
